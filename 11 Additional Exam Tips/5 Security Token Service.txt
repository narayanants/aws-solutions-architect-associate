Security Token Service:
------------------------

* Federation - Combining or joining list of users in one domain (IAM) to another domain (Facebook,Google,LinkedIn)
* Identity Broker - Identity from A to B
* Identity Store - Active Directory,Facebook
* Identities - user of a service like Facebook.
