
Aurora:
======

* Starts in 10 GB, scales in 10GB increments to 64TB. (Storage Autoscaling)
* Compute resource can scale upto 32 vCPU and 244 GB of RAM.
* Maintains 2 copies of DB in 3 AZ. 6 copies of data.
* Loss of 3 copies of data.
* Aurora storage is self-healing.


Replicas:
========

    * Aurora Replicas (currently 15) - Automatically Failover for Read Replica
    * MySQL Read Replicas (Current 5) - Not supported.
