    ElasticCache:
    ==============

    * Web service to deploy,operate and scale an in-memory cache in the cloud.
    * ElasticCache supports two types of open-source in-memory cache engines:

        * Memcached
        * Redis

    * Improves performance of web application by allowing you to retrieve information from fast,managed, in-memory caches, instead of relying on disk-based databases.
