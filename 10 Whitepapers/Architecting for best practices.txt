Architecting for Best Practices:
================================

 Business Benefit of Cloud:
 --------------------------

* Almost 0 upfront infra investment
* Just in time infrastructure
* More efficient resource utilization.
* Usage based costing
* Reduced time to market.

Technical benefits of cloud:
============================

* automation - scriptable infrastructure
* Autoscaling
* Proactive scaling
* More efficient dev cycle.
* DR and Business Continuity.
* Overflow the traffic to the cloud.


Always design for failure.
Decouple your compnenets.

Proactive Event based scaling: Big surge in traffic.
