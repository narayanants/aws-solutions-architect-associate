NAT vs Bastions:
=================

    * Bastions are used for administration only.
    * NAT Gateway are now used in Enterprise.

==========

    * Bastion is used to securely administer EC2 instances(using SSH or RDP) in private subnets. Jumpboxes in Austraila.
